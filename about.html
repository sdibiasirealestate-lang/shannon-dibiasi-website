Meet Shannon

Warm, strategic,
and genuinely local.

Shannon DiBiasi is a former 104.7 KISS FM radio personality turned top-producing real estate agent serving Gilbert and the surrounding East Valley.

Originally from Colorado, she's lived across the Phoenix metro and chose Gilbert to raise her family — making her a go-to guide for clients doing the same. With a focus on relocations, first-time buyers, VA loans, and new construction, she pairs real-life experience with deep local knowledge to help families find community and long-term value.

As a mom of two and a Morrison Ranch resident, Shannon brings a warm, approachable, and highly strategic approach to every transaction.

Morrison Ranch.

The complete guide to Gilbert's most beloved community — the villages, the homes, the schools, the lakes, and the lifestyle. Written by someone who actually lives here.

What is Morrison Ranch?

Morrison Ranch is a master-planned residential community on the east side of Gilbert, Arizona, generally around Higley and Elliot Roads (zip codes 85296 and 85234). It's best known for its unmistakable look: tree-lined streets, white ranch-style fencing, deep front porches, greenbelts, catch-and-release lakes, ramadas, and the iconic water towers that mark the community.

It was designed with a New England farmhouse sensibility and a clear intention — to feel like a real neighborhood, where evenings are slow, kids play outside, and neighbors actually know each other. That's rare in a metro this size, and it's a big part of why Morrison Ranch homes hold their value.

The Villages

Morrison Ranch is several neighborhoods in one.

Most people say "Morrison Ranch" like it's one place. It's really a collection of distinct villages, each with its own age of homes, feel, and price point. Knowing the difference is the key to buying well here.

01

Lakeview Trails

North & South. The lakes, the trails, the postcard Morrison Ranch lifestyle front and center.

02

Higley Groves

The established heart of the Ranch — mature trees, settled streets, a little more home for your money.

03

The Orchards

Smaller and tucked away, with a quieter, close-knit feel. Charm and walkability over square footage.

04

Elliot Groves

One of the newer additions — fresher builds and floor plans without taking on an older home.

05

Warner Groves

Larger farmhouse-style floor plans, popular with move-up buyers wanting space and the Ranch look.

06

Heritage District

The community's charming commercial pocket — offices and services wrapped in the same farmhouse style.

Homes & Real Estate

What homes cost in Morrison Ranch.

Homes in Morrison Ranch are primarily single-family, farmhouse- and ranch-inspired, built largely from the early 2000s through today, and ranging from roughly 2,000 to well over 5,000 square feet.

As of 2026, prices generally run from the high $500,000s into the $1.3 million+ range depending on the village, the lot, and how updated the home is. The spread between the entry and top end is bigger than most buyers expect — which is exactly why the village and the lot matter as much as the price. Turnkey homes in the best pockets tend to sell fast.

Schools

Family-first, and it shows.

Morrison Ranch is served by highly regarded Gilbert-area public schools, primarily within Gilbert Public Schools and Higley Unified School District. Because the community spans multiple villages, exact school assignment depends on the specific home's address — always worth confirming for the property you're considering. Strong schools are a major reason families put down roots here.

Amenities & Lifestyle

Lakes, trails, and porch-life.

Residents enjoy catch-and-release fishing lakes, miles of walking and biking trails, greenbelts, ramadas and picnic areas, playgrounds, and year-round community events — food-truck nights, seasonal gatherings, and the kind of front-porch culture the neighborhood was built around. It's genuinely walkable and made for being outside.

Location & Commute

Central to everything the East Valley offers.

Morrison Ranch sits minutes from the Loop 202 (Santan Freeway) and US-60, making the rest of the East Valley and greater Phoenix easy to reach — roughly 30–40 minutes to Phoenix Sky Harbor Airport. You're close to Downtown Gilbert's Heritage District, SanTan Village shopping, and some of the best dining in the Valley, including nearby Agritopia. It's the rare community that feels tucked away but connected.

Frequently Asked

Morrison Ranch questions, answered.

Where is Morrison Ranch located?

On the east side of Gilbert, Arizona, generally around Higley and Elliot Roads (zip codes 85296 and 85234), minutes from the Loop 202 and US-60.

What are the villages of Morrison Ranch?

Several distinct villages, including Lakeview Trails (North & South), Higley Groves, The Orchards, Elliot Groves, and Warner Groves — each with its own feel, home age, and price range.

What do homes cost in Morrison Ranch?

As of 2026, generally the high $500,000s to $1.3 million+, depending on the village, lot, size, and condition. Turnkey homes in the best villages sell quickly.

What schools serve Morrison Ranch?

Highly rated Gilbert-area public schools, primarily within Gilbert Public Schools and Higley Unified School District. Assignment depends on the exact address.

Does Morrison Ranch have an HOA?

Yes — it maintains the greenbelts, lakes, and common areas and hosts community events. Dues vary by village, so confirm the current amount for the specific home.

Is Morrison Ranch a good place to live?

It's one of the most sought-after communities in Gilbert — loved for its look, its schools, its family-friendly amenities, and its central East Valley location.
